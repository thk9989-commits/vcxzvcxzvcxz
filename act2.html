SCENARIO

When customers arrive at a drop-in advice centre: • They collect a three-digit ticket number (001,002,003…) • Customers are seen in order • Appointments last 5–10 minutes • No time is lost • Advisers work 09:00 – 12:00 and 13:00 onward • Lunch 12:00 – 13:00 • Always enough customers Rachel visits Monday. Only ONE adviser working. Number 028 is displayed.

QUESTIONS

A) What is the earliest possible time?
B) What is the latest possible time?

D) Highest possible ticket at 11:45?
