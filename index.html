SCENARIO:

When customers arrive at a drop-in advice centre, they collect a ticket with a three-digit number: 001, 002, 003, etc. Customers are seen in order of arrival. When an adviser is free, the next number is displayed. Appointments last between 5 and 10 minutes. All advisers start at 09:00 and take lunch from 12:00 – 13:00. Rachel visits on Monday. Only one adviser is working. Number 028 is displayed.
1) Rachel sees 028. What is the earliest possible arrival time?
2) If Sam sees three advisers, what is the latest possible start time?
3) Tommy (063) displayed after 048 — smallest waiting time?
4) Tommy (063) — largest waiting time?
5) Mean wait time for Tommy?
