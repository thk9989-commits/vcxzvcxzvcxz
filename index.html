SCENARIO

When customers arrive at a drop-in advice centre: • They collect a three-digit ticket number (001, 002, 003, etc). • Customers are seen in order of arrival. • When an adviser is free, the next number is displayed. • The number stays on the display until the next adviser is free. • Each appointment lasts between 5 and 10 minutes. • No time is lost between appointments. • There are always enough customers. • All advisers start at 09:00. • Lunch break is 12:00 – 13:00. Rachel visits on Monday. Only ONE adviser is working. Number **028** is displayed.
1) Rachel sees number 028. What is the earliest possible arrival time?
2) If Sam sees three advisers, what is the latest possible appointment start time?
3) Tommy (063) is shown after 048. What is the smallest possible waiting time?
4) Tommy (063) — what is the largest possible waiting time?
5) What is the mean (average) waiting time for Tommy?
